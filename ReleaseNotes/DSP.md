# 3.3 Release Notes: DSP

**Release 3.3** — Copyright ©1995 by NeXT Computer, Inc. All Rights Reserved.

All DSP development tools, with the exception of the assembler, linker, and librarian have been unbundled from NEXTSTEP. The DSP utilities (including source) as well as the Music Kit library should be available via anonymous ftp from `ccrma-ftp.stanford.edu`. (CCRMA is Stanford's Center for Computer Research in Music and Acoustics.)
